# Preface

Since the 1980s, I've been deeply involved in the development of commercial Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools and applications. My journey has led me to deliver AI systems and conduct research for for organizations like Capital One, Google, SAIC, DARPA, Olive AI, PacBell, and Babylist.

**Why is this book important to you?**

Too many developers and knowledge workers have started using Large Language Models (LLMS) without really understanding how they work, what their limitations are, and how best to use them for practical applications and as personal knowledge worker assitants.

My duel goal is to intertwine a solid foundation of machine learning and LLMs with practical techniques for using AI effectively in your job.

The landscape of AI has dramatically shifted. What was once the domain of physicists and computer scientists is now accessible to everyone. The latest commercial and open-source AI tools are designed with non-technical knowledge workers in mind. This democratization means you can leverage AI to enhance your productivity, creativity, and efficiency—no programming required.

As human beings, we don't live to work; we work to live. Our ultimate goal is to spend quality time with friends and family and engage in activities that nourish our souls. This book addresses a vital question:

**How can we be more efficient in our work so we have more time for what truly matters?**

There has always been important decisions in out lives concerning work-life balance. Most of us felt a pull towards maximising our earning potential and our careers while wanting to spend quality time with oue family and friends.

AI technologies hold out a promise of greatly increased productivity. As I write this in April 2025 CEOs and founders of AI companies are spreadi g a lot of hype about the death of careee software development, customer service, and other jobs 

**What this book offers**

This book is your guide to navigating the exciting world of AI tools, even if you have little prior programming experience. I do expect that you, dear reader, have some experince with the Python language but this book is intended to be self-contained: if you can edit a Python source file, install libraries, and run Python scripts then you are good to go with the material in this book. This book is structured into two main parts:

1. **AI Tool Use for Non-Programmers**

We'll explore various AI applications you can start using immediately to streamline your work processes. Whether you're in marketing, finance, education, or any other field, there are AI tools that can make your job easier and more fulfilling. You'll learn how to:

   - Automate repetitive tasks.
   - Generate insights from data.
   - Enhance your decision-making capabilities.
   - Improve communication with natural language processing.

2. **Python Programming Examples**

Python examples are provided at the end of most chapters. These sections delve deeper into how AI tools work under the hood and how you can customize them. Don't worry if you're not a programmer—these sections are entirely optional and are there for readers who wish to explore further.

**Why AI is for everyone**

The advent of user-friendly AI tools means you don't need to be a technical expert to benefit from AI. For example:

- **Natural Language Processing**: Draft reports or emails more quickly.
- **AI-Driven Analytics**: Uncover insights hidden in complex data sets.
- **Image Recognition**: Organize and search through visual content efficiently.
- **Automation Tools**: Free up time by automating routine tasks.

The possibilities are endless, and this book aims to open those doors for you.

**A note on the use of AI in this book**

In writing this book, I've embraced the very tools I'm discussing. While most of the text is my own, I've used Large Language Models (LLMs) like Llama 3, ChatGPT, Microsoft's CoPilot, Perplexity, Abacus.AI, and Claude to brainstorm ideas and assist in drafting some sections. LLMs can sometimes produce inaccurate information, so I always verify and edit any AI-generated content to ensure accuracy and align it with my voice.
